EvoAI is a C++17 library to evolve and / or train **small** Neural Networks.

Features
--------

- A :class:`Genome<EvoAI::Genome>` to use NEAT and HyperNEAT algorithms to evolve :class:`Neural Networks<EvoAI::NeuralNetwork>` .
- A Population(with speciation) to evolve your own :class:`Genomes<EvoAI::Genome>` , it only needs to fulfill :func:`meta::is_populable_v\<MyClass\><EvoAI::meta::is_populable_v>`
- Common :class:`Selection Algorithms<EvoAI::SelectionAlgorithms>` and optimizers (:class:`SGD<EvoAI::SGD>` and :class:`Adam<EvoAI::Adam>`).
- Train neural networks using backpropagation, enabling them to learn from labeled datasets.
